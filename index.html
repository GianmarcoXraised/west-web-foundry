FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

Do I own my website and domain?

Yes. The domain is registered in your name and the content is yours. If you ever leave, we hand everything over cleanly — no hostage situations.

How long does it take to go live?

Start and Growth sites usually launch in 2–4 weeks. Commerce and Custom projects typically take 4–8 weeks depending on scope. You'll get a clear timeline on the first call.

What if I need changes after launch?

That's exactly what the care plan is for. Email us the edit and we'll make it — text, images, prices, new sections. Each package includes a monthly edit allowance, and bigger changes are quoted upfront.

I already have a domain / an old website. Can you use it?

Absolutely. We'll transfer or connect your existing domain at no extra cost, and we can migrate content from your old site if it's worth keeping.

Are there any hidden costs?

No. The one-off fee covers design and build; the monthly fee covers domain, hosting, security and edits. The only extras are things you ask for — like additional pages or new features — and they're always quoted before we start.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes, and most clients do. You only pay the difference between packages, and your monthly plan changes with it. Your site is built so it can grow without starting over.
